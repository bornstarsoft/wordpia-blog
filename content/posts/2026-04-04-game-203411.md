Get ready for a wild ride with "Good Luck," a physics-based urban survival game launching on April 8, 2026, for PC, with later releases for PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S. Developed by the independent Turkish studio Happybara Games, this game promises a unique blend of chaotic gameplay and humor as players navigate a city filled with absurd traps and unpredictable hazards. With single-player and online co-op modes for up to five players, its designed to test your skills and patience in a world where anything can go wrong.

In "Good Luck," players will find themselves in a third-person arcade platformer where the goal is to reach a meeting while dodging everything from banana peels to trash cans that can lead to hilarious failures. The absence of checkpoints adds to the challenge, making every playthrough a fresh experience filled with unexpected twists. The game shines in co-op mode, where teamwork is essential to survive the urban jungle and tackle the chaos together.

As we await the release, its exciting to think about how "Good Luck" will redefine urban survival gaming. Will you be ready to face the madness with friends or go solo in this unpredictable adventure?
