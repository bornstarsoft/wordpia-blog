If you enjoy puzzle games that strike a balance between challenge and accessibility, then Botany Manor may just be your new favorite. Currently available for free on the Epic Games Store, this cozy first-person puzzle game invites players to step into the shoes of botanist Arabella Greene, who explores a Victorian manor to complete her botanical research book. Unlike other notoriously challenging puzzle games, Botany Manor aims to provide a relaxing experience without the frustration of overly complex puzzles.

The developers have crafted a game that encourages exploration and curiosity, with puzzles designed to be engaging but not overwhelming. Lead developer Laure De May emphasizes the importance of a peaceful setting and a balanced challenge, ensuring that players can enjoy the journey without feeling inadequate. With positive reviews highlighting its appeal to casual gamers, Botany Manor is a perfect fit for those looking for a delightful puzzle experience.

As this game is free for a limited time, it presents a fantastic opportunity to dive into a world of botanical mysteries. Will you take the plunge and explore the serene puzzles of Botany Manor? The adventure awaits!
