In a significant move to enhance its global gaming presence, JetSynthesys has acquired the popular merge puzzle game EverMerge, which has impressively generated over $350 million in lifetime revenue. This acquisition marks a pivotal step in JetSynthesys' strategy to create a worldwide gaming ecosystem, particularly as it aims to tap into high-growth markets like India. With EverMerge's established success in Western markets, JetSynthesys is poised to leverage its expertise in community engagement to introduce the game to new audiences.

Rajan Navani, Chairman & MD of JetSynthesys, highlighted the importance of this acquisition, stating that it represents a milestone in recognizing the potential of the orange economy in India. The company plans to bridge global intellectual properties with local insights, enhancing EverMerge's appeal through culturally relevant storytelling and innovative technology. As JetSynthesys continues to invest in EverMerge, players can expect fresh content and engaging experiences that evolve over time.

This acquisition not only reinforces JetSynthesys' commitment to its player community but also accelerates its journey toward becoming a leading global games company. With the gaming industry continuously evolving, it will be interesting to see how JetSynthesys navigates this new chapter and what innovative strategies they will implement to captivate players worldwide.
