Dinosaur Polo Club has taken a bold step back to their roots with the release of RTFM, a bite-sized co-op puzzle game that showcases the creativity sparked during their game jam weeks. This unique title, available for free on PC and Mac, invites players to navigate the surreal challenges of a fictional corporate environment while solving puzzles and maintaining trust with their partner. The game cleverly combines elements of social sleuthing and cooperative gameplay, reminiscent of popular titles like Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes.

With RTFM, players embody two roles: the Troubleshooter and the Terminal Operator, working together to decode puzzles and avoid a chaotic meltdown. The games design encourages communication and collaboration, making it not just a test of skill but also of teamwork. As players dive into this quirky experience, theyll find themselves immersed in a narrative that blends humor with the tension of corporate life.

As we look forward to more innovative projects from Dinosaur Polo Club, RTFM serves as a reminder of the magic that can happen when creativity is given the freedom to flourish. What other surprises might emerge from their next Creativity Week?
