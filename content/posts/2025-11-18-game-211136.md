In a world where gaming often revolves around high-stakes action and intense competition, **The Cute Whale** offers a refreshing alternative. This charming logic adventure invites players to embark on a peaceful journey through the ocean, guiding a lovable whale as it helps lost turtles find their way home. With its soothing gameplay and delightful visuals, this game is a perfect escape for those seeking a moment of zen.

The Cute Whale features 30 handcrafted puzzles across three vibrant biomes: the sunny Caribbean Sea, the colorful Coral Reef, and the mysterious Deep Sea. Each level challenges players to think strategically while enjoying the calming ambiance of the ocean. The game is designed to be played at your own pace, making it ideal for puzzle enthusiasts looking for a relaxing experience.

Available for just 4.19 on Xbox One, Xbox Series X|S, and Windows PC, The Cute Whale is not just a game; it's an invitation to restore harmony to the sea. As you push turtles onto buoys and solve each stage, youll find yourself immersed in a world where creativity and tranquility coexist. Will you take the plunge and help the turtles find their way home?
