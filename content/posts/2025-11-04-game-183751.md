In an exciting turn of events for Nintendo fans, both Mario Kart World and Donkey Kong Bananza have been shortlisted for the prestigious 'Console Game of the Year' at the Golden Joystick Awards. This recognition not only highlights the quality of these titles but also underscores the strong start of the Switch 2's game library, which has already garnered multiple nominations in various categories.

The competition is fierce, with both games facing off against notable titles such as Death Stranding 2 and Ghost of Ytei. Mario Kart World is also in the running for 'Best Multiplayer Game', while Donkey Kong Bananza shines in the 'Best Audio Design' category. As the voting period opens, fans are eager to support their favorites and see which games will take home the coveted awards on November 21, 2025.

As we look forward to the announcement of the winners, its clear that Nintendo continues to innovate and captivate audiences with its game offerings. Will Mario Kart World and Donkey Kong Bananza emerge victorious, or will another title steal the spotlight? Only time will tell!
