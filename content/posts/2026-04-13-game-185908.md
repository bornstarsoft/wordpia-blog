Hideo Kojima's upcoming game, Physint, is generating buzz with its recent casting call that hints at a thrilling hijacked bus scene. This new project, codenamed 'Shimmer', aims to redefine stealth gaming with a fresh narrative and a villain reminiscent of Mads Mikkelsen's iconic roles. The casting call, which seeks diverse characters, suggests a rich storyline that could captivate players for years to come.

As Kojima Productions gears up for production, slated to begin in June, fans are left wondering how this new title will evolve. With Kojima himself stating that Physint could take five to six years to develop, anticipation is building for what promises to be a groundbreaking addition to the PlayStation lineup. Will this new IP live up to the legacy of Death Stranding? Only time will tell as we await further revelations from Kojima and his team.
