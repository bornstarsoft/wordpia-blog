In an intriguing turn of events, Walmart may have inadvertently revealed two upcoming GameCube classics for the Nintendo Switch Online service: Metroid Prime 2 and Pikmin 2. This leak, spotted by vigilant social media users, showcases a selection of games on Walmart's website, hinting at potential additions to the already exciting lineup of titles.

While Nintendo has officially confirmed three GameCube gamesSuper Mario Sunshine, Pokmon XD: Gale of Darkness, and Pokmon Colosseumno release dates have been provided. The inclusion of Metroid Prime 2 and Pikmin 2 raises questions about whether this is merely a mistake or an early glimpse into Nintendo's plans for the service. With the recent remaster of the original Metroid Prime and the standalone HD releases of the first two Pikmin games, fans are eager for more content.

As the Nintendo Switch Online Expansion Pack continues to grow, priced at $49.99 annually, players are left wondering what other surprises Nintendo might have in store. Will we see more GameCube classics make their way to the platform? Only time will tell!
