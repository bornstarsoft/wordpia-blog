The excitement surrounding the gaming community is palpable with the announcement of Opus Magnum: Complete Edition for Nintendo Switch, set to launch on March 17, 2026. This edition not only brings the acclaimed open-ended puzzle game to a new platform but also includes the prequel, De Re Metallica, enriching the experience for both new and returning players.

Developed by Zachtronics, known for their innovative puzzle games, Opus Magnum challenges players to master intricate machinery and solve complex alchemical puzzles. With features like open-ended gameplay, a rich narrative filled with intrigue, and even a solitaire minigame, this title promises to captivate players' minds and creativity. As we await its release, one can't help but wonder how this new edition will enhance the already beloved gameplay mechanics.

Are you ready to dive back into the world of alchemy and engineering? The Complete Edition is shaping up to be a must-have for puzzle enthusiasts and Switch owners alike.
