Konami is set to delight fans with a nostalgic 13-game Goemon collection, launching on July 2 for Nintendo Switch, PS5, and Steam. This compilation features beloved titles from the Famicom, Super Famicom, and Game Boy eras, bringing back the whimsical adventures of the titular character, Goemon. While currently exclusive to Japan, the collection promises to introduce new features like rewind, quick save, and a music player, enhancing the classic gaming experience.

The collection includes iconic games such as "The Legend of the Mystical Ninja" and "Ganbare Goemon 3," showcasing the series' unique humor and pop culture parodies. With both digital and physical editions available, including a limited-edition package, this release is a must-have for retro gaming enthusiasts. Will Konami consider a global release in the future? Only time will tell!
